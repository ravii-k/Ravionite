Before You Start

Use the collection like a workflow, not a grab bag.

The prompts are designed to stack. Start with intake, move into analysis, and end with a plain-language truth test. That sequence is what makes the system work.

⚠
Advanced Models Only
These prompts are structurally demanding. They rely on multi-step reasoning, careful synthesis, and strict instruction-following. Basic or free-tier models will usually flatten the output and miss the point of the framework.
